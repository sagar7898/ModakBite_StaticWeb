Why Ukadiche Modak?

Ukadiche Modak is considered the favorite sweet of Lord Ganesha 🙏. During Ganesh Chaturthi, these delicious steamed modaks are offered as prasad to seek blessings and prosperity.

Made using rice flour, coconut, and jaggery, Ukadiche Modak represents purity and traditional Maharashtrian culture. The soft outer covering with a sweet filling inside makes it a perfect festive delight.

At Modak Bite, we prepare every modak with love ❤️ and authentic ingredients to give you the true taste of tradition. Whether it's Ukadiche, Chocolate, or Dry Fruit Modak — every bite is special.

Celebrate this Ganpati festival with fresh homemade modaks and bring joy to your family and loved ones.
